Lahmacun

Description

Lahmacun is a common dish in turkey made from dough, mince meat, onions and tomatoes Pretty similar to a mince meat pie or pizza. Its main attraction is the way its eaten. Lahmacun is basically a tortilla roll thats crunchy and meaty and salty. Inside people commonly use lettuce, onions, tomatoes, oregano, lemon juice.

Standard bread dough is made and stretched to shape roughly 40cm wide. Making it a bit oval will help when doing the roll and its the traditional shape used for long closed ovens. The mince meat mix is made with garlic onions tomatoes and beef with salt, pepper, cumin, hot pepper added for spices. After heating the dough for 10-15 minutes the mix is added on gently spreading and letting the dough envelop some. The juice from tomato and garlic will seep into the dough making it tastier.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Minced Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Hot Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Lemon or Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Make Dough
  2. Make mix
  3. Bake dough for 10 mins
  4. Add mix by pressing on dough
  5. Bake 20-30 mins
  6. Add your preffered ingredients
  7. Roll and enjoy
